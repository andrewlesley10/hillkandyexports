Exporter FAQ.

What are your primary export markets?

We specialize in serving the hospitality and retail sectors in the Maldives and the Gulf region, including the UAE, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia. However, we are equipped to ship globally.

Do you provide quality certifications?

Yes. Every shipment is accompanied by the necessary phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, and quality analysis reports as required by the destination country's regulations.

What is the typical lead time?

Lead times vary based on the product and destination. Generally, orders are processed and ready for shipment within 7-14 business days from the confirmation of payment.

Can you handle custom packaging?

Absolutely. We offer customized bulk packaging and private labeling services for hotels, restaurants, and retail brands looking for premium Sri Lankan products under their own identity.
