About Us.

Founded on 31 March 2017 in the royal city of Kandy, Hill Kandy Exporters (Private) Limited (ROC: PV-121299) was established to bring the finest treasures of Sri Lanka to the world. Our heritage is rooted in the rich agricultural traditions of the highlands, where we work directly with master planters and ethical producers to source only the highest grades of spices, coconut derivatives, and tea.

We believe in transparency, quality, and the preservation of authentic Sri Lankan flavors. From our Kandy headquarters, we coordinate a sophisticated network that ensures every shipment meets the stringent standards of the international market.
